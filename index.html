workflow

1. procurement and providing a better tomorrow

We focus and depend primarily on the low level milk producing farmers tocomplete our orders that we receive from our customers . we make sure that the farmers get their fair share of profit and benefits from doing business with our company if they are enrolled in our subscription service

2. the trident - distribution channels

2.1 First and foremost priority Our first branch in our distribution tree focuses on completing bulk orders from milk processing facilitieswhich require large volume of milk for their daily purposes. our aim in the first distribution branch is to fulfill the requirements of milk processing plants before any other oorders as this pathway results in quick profit and minimal wastage.

2.2 Waste mitigation once the first priority is fulfilled , we focus on businesses that require milk for their product maufacturing process. The main objective of this channel is helping us in reducing our wastages and improving our profits as these businesses usually require small volume of milk . the leftover milk in the inventory at the end of the day can be sold to these businesses for quick profit and low level of loss due to unsold inventory

2.3 The welcomed ones after fulfilling the needs in our two distribution channels , we finally turn our attention to our vision and providing a better tomorrow for the society . we sell our procurements to non profit organisation and health centres at a discounted price . we sell our procurements at discounted price to NGOs and we retain this loss by embedding the NGOs to our subscription service.

3. subscription service - our holy grail

Our elite subscription service is available to our all customers starting from low level milk producer from whom we procure our milk to the businesses and NGOs that buy our milk. the secret auce in our subscription service is that members enrolled will be rewarded with points which they can accumulate by being a consistent and relaible customer to our company . the fdamers and milk producers can use ther points to avail various options such as free cattle health checkup , for buying products from businesses ivolved in our distribution channel , free fodder feed for the cattles and to avail the services of a NGO for help. the businesses in our second distribution channel can redeem points by choosing options like marketing purposes , hiring cheaps staffs which will be provided by our NGOs. thw NGOs use the subscription to buy products from us and the businesses at a discounted price. in this way , our subscription service creates oppurtunity and profit for all parties involved with us.
